How to become a volunteer for the RSPCA NSW

    1
  • Learn about how you can become a volunteer for RSPCA NSW. You must be older than 16 years old to volunteer with us.
    • 2
  • Choose the volunteer roles that are right for you, using location, interest/specialty, and time commitment.
    • 2
  • Enter your contact details and upload your CV in order for RSPCA to get in touch with you. You will recieve a follow up email with a list of the available in-person training times and links to online inductions for your selected roles.
    • 4
  • Show up for your first day of volunteering with RSPCA NSW!
